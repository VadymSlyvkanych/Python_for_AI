Промпт-инжиниринг для Stable Diffusion / Gemini Image

1. Ключевые техники

   a) Структура промпта
      [Subject] + [Action/Setting] + [Style/Medium] + [Lighting] +
      [Colors] + [Composition] + [Quality Boosters]

   b) Quality Boosters
      - "highly detailed", "intricate details", "sharp focus"
      - "8k", "4k", "ultra HD"
      - "masterpiece", "best quality", "artstation trending"

   c) Style Modifiers
      - "cinematic lighting", "volumetric lighting", "dramatic lighting"
      - "photorealistic", "hyperrealistic", "digital art", "oil painting"
      - "unreal engine 5", "octane render", "ray tracing"
      - "by greg rutkowski", "by studio ghibli", "artstation"

   d) Negative Prompts (чего избегать)
      - "nsfw, low quality, blurry, distorted, ugly, bad anatomy"
      - "extra limbs, deformed, poorly drawn face"

   e) Weighting (акцентирование)
      - Повторение важных слов: "cat cat cat"
      - Расстановка приоритетов: сначала subject, потом окружение

2. Примеры применения

   Простой промпт:
     "a cat sitting on a chair"

   С применением техник:
     "a fluffy orange cat sitting on an antique wooden chair, soft
     afternoon sunlight streaming through window, photorealistic,
     highly detailed, intricate fur texture, shallow depth of field,
     cinematic composition, 8k, masterpiece"
